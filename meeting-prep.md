\# Client Meeting Prep



\## Before the meeting

\- Confirm the exact problem the client wants solved, in their words

\- Pull relevant history (past tickets, prior module talks)

\- Prepare a short demo path mapped to their stated need

\- Decide what I want to walk out with (a next step counts)



\## During the meeting

\- Open by restating their goal

\- Demo only what serves that goal

\- Capture questions I cannot answer on the spot instead of guessing



\## Pricing and recommendations

\- Lay out options with trade-offs; final call stays with me
